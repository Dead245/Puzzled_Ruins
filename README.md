# Puzzled Ruins

This Hytale asset pack was created within about ~72 hours during the 7-day modjam for Hytale that was hosted in June 2026.

Explore a crumbling ruin filled with puzzles, secrets, and hidden treasure.

Deep within the ruins lies a pile of treasure sealed behind an ancient mechanism. To unlock it, you must explore the structure, solve eight unique puzzles, and collect the decorated gold blocks hidden throughout the ruins.

Once all eight gold blocks have been recovered, place them within the central podium to reveal the final treasure chamber.

Bring in some friends to solve the puzzles together!

***(Warning: some of the puzzles can be a tad difficult)***

## Features

- 8 unique puzzle rooms
- Central hallway connecting all puzzle areas
- Final treasure chamber unlocked by completing every puzzle

## Estimated Playtime

30–60 minutes

## How to Play

1. Enter the ruins via the portal key fragment or /instance -- The portal key has a time limit of 90 minutes while within it
   The key is also craftable with Cobalt and Yellow Crystal Fragments.
3. Complete puzzles to earn decorated gold blocks
4. Place collected blocks in the central podium
5. Unlock the final chamber and claim the treasure
6. Return portal is at the spawn point for if you used a portal key.
